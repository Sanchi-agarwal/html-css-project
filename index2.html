Heart Attacks Among Youngsters

by Dr. Ananya Verma • July 05, 2025

Why Are Young People at Risk?

Heart attacks were once considered a health issue for the elderly. But in recent years, more cases have been reported among people under 40. This alarming trend calls for greater awareness and preventive action.

Major Causes

Several lifestyle and environmental factors contribute to this rise in cardiovascular events:

  • High stress levels from work or studies
  • Poor diet rich in processed and fast foods
  • Lack of physical activity
  • Smoking and alcohol abuse
  • Undiagnosed genetic factors or pre-existing conditions

Prevention Tips

  • Adopt a heart-healthy diet (rich in vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains)
  • Exercise regularly – at least 30 minutes a day
  • Limit stress through yoga, mindfulness, or hobbies
  • Go for annual health checkups
  • Quit smoking and moderate alcohol intake
“Your heart is precious — take care of it while you're still young.”

Early awareness and healthy habits can make a big difference in preventing heart attacks, even in your 20s or 30s.
